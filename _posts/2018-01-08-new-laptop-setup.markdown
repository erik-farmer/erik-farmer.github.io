<ul>

    <li>Firefox</li>
    <li>iTerm</li>
    <li>zsh</li>
    <li>Sublime Text</li>
    <li>JetBrains Toolbox</li>
    <li>XCode (developer tools)</li>
    <li>homebrew</li>
    <li>python3</li>
    <li>Node</li>
    <li>Slack</li>
    <li>Skype (if I'm interviewing)</li>
    <li>Docker for Mac (I generally use this for local MySQL/Postgres as well)</li>
</ul>
